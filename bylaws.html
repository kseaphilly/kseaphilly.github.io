Bylaws of the KSEA Philadelphia Chapter

CHAPTER 1: GENERAL

ARTICLE 1. Name

The name of this organization shall be the Philadelphia Chapter of the Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA), hereinafter referred to as "KSEA Philadelphia".

ARTICLE 2. Purpose

The purposes of KSEA Philadelphia are:

  • To promote the application of science and technology for the general welfare of society.
  • To foster international cooperation, especially between the United States and Korea.
  • To help Korean-American scientists and engineers develop their full career potential.
  • To serve the community by delivering scientific and technological information.

ARTICLE 3. Non-Profit Organization

KSEA Philadelphia shall be a non-profit organization. No part of its net earnings shall inure to the benefit of any private individual.

ARTICLE 4. Territory

The territory of KSEA Philadelphia shall cover the Greater Philadelphia area, including Southeastern Pennsylvania, Southern New Jersey, and Delaware.

CHAPTER 2: MEMBERSHIP

ARTICLE 5. Qualification

Membership in KSEA Philadelphia is open to any person who subscribes to the purposes of KSEA Philadelphia and meets the qualifications of one of the membership grades.

ARTICLE 6. Membership Grades

The membership grades shall be: Regular Member, Student Member, and Honorary Member.

  • Regular Member: A person who holds a bachelor's degree or higher in science, engineering, or a related field.
  • Student Member: A student who is enrolled in a college or university and majoring in science, engineering, or a related field.
  • Honorary Member: A person who has made significant contributions to KSEA Philadelphia or the Korean-American community.

ARTICLE 7. Dues

Members shall pay annual dues as determined by the Council. Honorary Members are exempt from dues.

ARTICLE 8. Rights and Privileges

Regular Members in good standing shall have the right to vote and hold office. Student Members shall have the right to vote but not hold office, except for the Student Representative position.

CHAPTER 3: ORGANIZATION

ARTICLE 9. General Assembly

The General Assembly shall consist of all voting members. It shall be the supreme governing body of KSEA Philadelphia.

ARTICLE 10. Council

The Council shall be the board of directors of KSEA Philadelphia. It shall consist of the Officers and Councilors.

ARTICLE 11. Councilors

Councilors shall be elected by the members. The number of Councilors shall be determined by the Council but shall not be less than five (5).

ARTICLE 12. Term of Office

The term of office for Officers and Councilors shall be one year, commencing on July 1 and ending on June 30 of the following year.

ARTICLE 13. Meetings

The General Assembly shall meet at least once a year. The Council shall meet at least quarterly.

ARTICLE 14. Quorum

A quorum for the General Assembly shall be ten percent (10%) of the voting members. A quorum for the Council shall be a majority of the Council members.

ARTICLE 15. Voting

Decisions shall be made by a majority of the votes cast, unless otherwise specified in these Bylaws.

ARTICLE 16. Salary

No stated salary shall be paid to Councilors for their services. However, a Councilor may serve KSEA Philadelphia in another capacity and receive reasonable compensation for those services.

CHAPTER 4: OFFICERS AND AUDITORS

ARTICLE 17. Officers

The officers of KSEA Philadelphia shall consist of a President, Vice President(s), an Executive Director (serving as Secretary), a Finance Director (serving as Treasurer), a General Director, a Membership Director, and other officers as appointed by the President.

ARTICLE 18. Duties

President: The President shall be the chief executive officer of KSEA Philadelphia, coordinate all offices and committees, and serve as Chair at Council and General Assembly meetings.

Vice President: The Vice President shall assist the President. An Executive Vice President, if designated, serves as President when the President is unable to fulfill the duties of the position.

Executive Director and Finance Director: They shall perform their duties under the direction of the President. The Executive Director shall maintain records of all meetings and serve as Secretary. The Finance Director shall be responsible for all financial matters of the chapter, including budgeting, accounting, and reporting, and serve as Treasurer.

ARTICLE 19. Auditors

Two Auditors shall be elected by the General Assembly. They shall audit KSEA Philadelphia's finances and report the results to the Council. An auditor may not simultaneously serve as an officer or Councilor.

CHAPTER 5: COMMITTEES

ARTICLE 20. Executive Committee

The Executive Committee shall consist of the President, Vice President, Executive Director, Finance Director, and Membership Director. It shall exercise the authority of the Council on matters including business plans, budget, and other items delegated by the Council.

ARTICLE 21. Other Committees

The Council may establish additional committees as needed.

CHAPTER 6: FINANCE

ARTICLE 22. Annual Reports

The final income and expense statement for the previous year and the business plan and budget for the current year shall be reported to all members after approval by the Council.

ARTICLE 23. Income and Expenses

KSEA Philadelphia has the following sources of income:

  • Membership fees.
  • Sponsorships, contributions, and donations from individuals and corporations.
  • Other income derived from KSEA Philadelphia activities.

ARTICLE 24. Fiscal Year

The fiscal year of KSEA Philadelphia shall commence on July 1 and end on June 30 of the following year.

ARTICLE 25. Bank Accounts

The Council shall designate one or more banks as depositories of KSEA Philadelphia funds and shall designate the officers authorized to conduct transactions.

ARTICLE 26. Indemnification

KSEA Philadelphia shall indemnify its Councilors and Officers to the fullest extent permitted by law.

ARTICLE 27. Dissolution

Upon the dissolution of the KSEA Philadelphia Chapter, any assets remaining after payment of or provision for its debts and liabilities shall be distributed for one or more exempt purposes within the meaning of section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, or shall be distributed to the federal government, or to a state or local government, for a public purpose. Preferably, such assets will be distributed to the national Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA), provided it is a section 501(c)(3) exempt organization at the time of dissolution.

CHAPTER 7: MISCELLANEOUS

ARTICLE 28. Offices

The KSEA Philadelphia Office shall be located in the Philadelphia area. The principal office may be changed from time to time upon approval of the Council.

ARTICLE 29. Amendment of Bylaws

Amendments to the Bylaws may be recommended by the Council and shall be submitted to the members at an annual or special meeting. Amendments shall require approval of at least two-thirds (2/3) of the total votes cast by a quorum of voting members.

ARTICLE 30. Effective Date

These Bylaws shall be effective immediately upon the approval of the General Assembly.
