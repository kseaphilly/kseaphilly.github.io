Membership

Join the KSEA Philadelphia Chapter to connect with a vibrant community of Korean-American scientists and engineers. As a member of KSEA, you can enjoy a wide range of activities and benefits.

Member Activities

  • Annual meetings and technical conferences (UKC)
  • National Mathematics Competition (for students in grades 4-11)
  • Young Generation programs and Katalyst
  • Publication of KSEA Letters
  • Scholarships and awards
  • Job referrals and professional networking
  • Technical evaluation and consulting opportunities
  • Topical symposia and workshops
  • Local chapter events and joint seminars
  • Science & technology programs for the general public

Membership Benefits

  • Leadership development through 13 Technical Groups and 14 Standing Committees
  • Networking with scientists and engineers from all fields across the U.S. and Korea
  • Opportunities for community service and mentoring
  • Access to information on professional opportunities and job openings
  • Office use at KSEA Headquarters while visiting Washington D.C.
  • Eligibility for KSEA Awards honoring distinguished records
  • Support for your local chapter (most dues go back to your own chapter)
