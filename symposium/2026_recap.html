February 27, 2026 UPenn Smilow Center

A Successful Convergence

Supported by KSEA and KUSCO, the 2026 US-Korea Northeastern Symposium successfully convened professionals, researchers, and students to discuss "Pioneering the Future: The Convergence of AI, Bio-Science, and Engineering."

The event featured inspiring keynote addresses from distinguished scholars, insightful presentations in precision medicine and future tech, and a vibrant poster session showcasing the innovative research of our community members.

We extend our deepest gratitude to all the organizing committee members, volunteers, and participants who contributed to this vibrant exchange of knowledge and networking. We look forward to continuing this momentum in our future events!
