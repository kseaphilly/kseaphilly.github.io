About KSEA Philadelphia Chapter

The KSEA Philadelphia Chapter is a vibrant community dedicated to advancing the careers and contributions of Korean-American scientists and engineers. We serve a broad region encompassing Pennsylvania (~80%), Delaware, and New Jersey, with a strategic concentration in the Philadelphia Core, the Main Line, King of Prussia, and surrounding suburbs. Our membership represents a dynamic mix of professionals from world-class academic institutions, leading pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies, and innovative tech startups.

Recently restored to full Chapter status, we are in an exciting phase of active growth and revitalization. We are committed to rebuilding a strong, interconnected community that bridges the gap between academia and industry. This symposium stands as a major milestone in our journey, designed to bring together our diverse members to foster meaningful collaboration, provide mentorship opportunities for the next generation, and drive professional growth across all disciplines. Our target is to raise $10,000 to fund key initiatives, primarily to support student travel and prestigious research awards.

Symposium Program

The 2026 US-Korea Northeastern Symposium on AI, Biomedical Science & Engineering, themed "Pioneering the Future: The Convergence of AI, Bio-Science, & Engineering," features a distinguished lineup of speakers from academia and industry. Our program is designed to foster cross-disciplinary dialogue and showcase cutting-edge research in life sciences, engineering, and technology.
