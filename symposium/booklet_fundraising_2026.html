Symposium Program

The 2026 US-Korea Northeastern Symposium on AI, Biomedical Science & Engineering, themed "Pioneering the Future: The Convergence of AI, Bio-Science, & Engineering," stands as a premier regional gathering. It features a distinguished lineup of speakers from top-tier academia and leading industries. Our program is meticulously designed to foster cross-disciplinary dialogue, showcase cutting-edge research in life sciences, engineering, and technology, and provide unparalleled networking opportunities for professionals and students alike.
