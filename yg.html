Young Generation (Student Chapters)

The Young Generation (YG) program supports Korean-American students in science and engineering fields. We collaborate with student chapters at local universities to provide networking, mentorship, and career development opportunities.

University of Pennsylvania

K-PEnSA (Korean Penn Scientists and Engineers Association)

K-PEnSA is the graduate student chapter of KSEA at the University of Pennsylvania. They organize academic seminars, social events, and networking sessions for graduate students and postdocs.
