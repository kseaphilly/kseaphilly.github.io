# Membership

Join the KSEA Philadelphia Chapter to connect with a vibrant community of Korean-American scientists and engineers. As a member of KSEA, you can enjoy a wide range of activities and benefits.

## Member Activities

*   Annual meetings and technical conferences
*   National Mathematics Competition (for students in grades 4-11)
*   Young Generation programs
*   Scholarships and awards
*   Job referrals
*   Technical evaluation and consulting opportunities
*   Local chapter events and networking

## Membership Benefits

*   Leadership development through committee activities
*   Networking with scientists and engineers from all fields
*   Opportunities for community service
*   Access to information on professional opportunities in the U.S. and Korea

## Join Us

Ready to become a member? Join KSEA through the national website. We look forward to welcoming you to our chapter!
