National Math and Science Competition (NMSC) 2026

Hosted by KSEA Philadelphia Chapter
* Note: We are hosting the Math Competition ONLY this year.

Math Competition General Information (NMC)

📅 Date & Time

April 18th 2026, SATURDAY

Starting at 2:00 PM EDT (Eastern Daylight Time)

📍 Location

TBD, University of Pennsylvania

Address: TBD

🎓 Qualification

4th to 11th grade students who attend school in the United States of America.

⏱️ Exam Format and Duration

Questions:

  • Part 1: 20 multiple-choice questions
  • Part 2: 5 free response questions with integer-valued answers

Time Limits:

  • 4th and 5th grade: 50 minutes all together (Part 1 and Part 2)
  • 6th to 11th grade: 70 minutes all together (Part 1 and Part 2)

📢 Chapter Notice

Please check the chapter website for further updates: https://philly.ksea.org/nmsc

⚠️ Non-Philadelphia Chapter Participants

Your child(ren)'s score will be sent to HQ separately for the National competition. However, your child(ren) is(are) not eligible to receive the Philadelphia Chapter award.
필라델피아 챕터가 아닌 다른 챕터에서 참가를 하는 경우, 아이의 성적은 본부로 전송되고 National level의 경쟁을 하게 됩니다. 하지만 필라델피아 챕터상은 받을 수 없습니다.

  • Chapter Selection: Please ensure you select the Philadelphia Chapter when registering. If you participate from another chapter territory, your score will be sent to the HQ for national competition, but you may not be eligible for local Philadelphia Chapter awards.
    (다른 챕터에서 참가하시는 경우 본부로 성적이 전송되어 전국 단위 경쟁은 가능하지만, 필라델피아 챕터 상은 받으실 수 없습니다.)
  • One Form Per Student: This online registration form is for "each" student. If you have multiple students in your family, you must submit a separate form per student.

  • Student Email Address: Please do not use parents' email address for the student email. You will receive the online Math exam link to this email address.
    (수학 시험 링크를 받기 위해 반드시 시험을 보는 학생 본인의 이메일을 사용해야 합니다.)
